My Favorite Books I've Read

Below are my all-time favorite reads. One of my favorite things to do is reading on a beach or by the lakes in Madison. Below are my top five books, and quick descriptions of them.

Big Little Lies
Written by Liane Moriarty, Big Little Lies highlights the "perfect" lives of wealthy families. Suddenly, the families are involved in a murder of one of their own.
The Woman in the Window
A.J. Finn's thriller novel shows us the perspective of Anna, a psychologist suffering from Agoraphobia. A new family moves in across the street, and Anna sees something she wasn't supposed to see, resulting in a downward spiral of her own life.
The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo
This book, written by Stieg Larsson, is a murder mystery following the disappearance of a girl 40 years ago. Her uncle hires a struggling journalist to help investigate, and he is accompanied by Lisbeth Salandar, or the girl with the large, dragon tattoo.
